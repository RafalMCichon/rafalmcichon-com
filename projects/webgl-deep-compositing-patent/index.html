Deep compositing in web browsers (patent)

TL;DR

I am a co-inventor of US11113858B2 — a system and method for deep compositing of images in web browsers (issued Sept 7, 2021).

What it enabled (high level)

The work focused on bringing deep compositing concepts into a browser-friendly pipeline, balancing:

  • performance constraints,
  • memory limits,
  • and real-time rendering requirements.
