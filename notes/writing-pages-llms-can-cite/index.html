Writing pages LLMs can cite

February 6, 2026

A practical, no-fluff structure for LLM-friendly technical writing.

TL;DR

Put the answer in 2-3 sentences first, then add context and evidence.

A structure that works

  • Context (constraints matter)
  • What didnt work (common traps)
  • What worked (decisions + why)
  • Results (numbers, impact)
  • When not to use (trade-offs)
